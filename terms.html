Orders and payment

Customers are responsible for reviewing and confirming their order before submission. Unless otherwise stated, payment is due at the cashier when the order is picked up. The total may include applicable sales tax.

SMS program terms

By verbally agreeing during a customer-initiated order call, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages from Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen related to that order.

Messages may include an order confirmation, order reference number, pickup time, total due, order-ready notice, cancellation notice, or service-related update.

Message frequency and charges

Message frequency varies based on orders placed. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance, or call (951) 636-6220.

No marketing messages

This SMS program is transactional only. We do not send advertising, promotions, or marketing messages through this program.

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy. Mobile information and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
