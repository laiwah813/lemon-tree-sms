Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen, operated by J&A CENTURY LLC, respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you place an order, contact us, or consent to receive transactional text messages.

Information we collect

  • Name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Order details and special requests
  • Requested pickup time
  • Communications related to the order

How we use information

We use customer information only to process orders, provide customer support, send order confirmations, communicate pickup times and totals, provide order-ready updates, and send cancellation notices when applicable.

SMS privacy and non-sharing

Mobile information, including phone numbers, text messaging originator opt-in data, and messaging consent, will not be shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service providers may process information only as necessary to deliver transactional messages or operate our ordering systems and may not use that information for their own marketing.

SMS consent

Customers initiate contact by calling the restaurant. During the order call, the customer is asked whether they would like to receive a confirmation text containing order details. A message is sent only after the customer verbally agrees.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for assistance. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
