Transactional order messaging

Fast, clear order communication.

Customers may call Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen to place pickup orders. With verbal permission, we may send transactional text messages containing an order reference number, pickup time, total due, order-ready update, or cancellation notice.

How the SMS program works

1. Customer calls

The customer initiates contact by calling the restaurant to place a pickup order.

2. Verbal consent

The customer is asked whether they want a confirmation text with order details.

3. Transactional updates

Only order-related messages are sent. We do not send marketing or promotional texts.

Mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS consent, is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
