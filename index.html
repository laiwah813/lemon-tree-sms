Pickup order updates

1. Call to order

Customers initiate contact by calling the restaurant and placing a pickup order.

2. Choose SMS updates

The ordering assistant asks whether the customer wants a confirmation text with order details.

3. Receive transactional updates

Messages may include the order number, pickup time, total due, readiness, or cancellation information.

We do not send marketing messages through this SMS program. Mobile information and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
