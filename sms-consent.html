SMS Program

This page explains how customers consent to and receive order-related text messages.

Who sends messages?

Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen, operated by J&A CENTURY LLC.

Who receives messages?

Only customers who call the restaurant, place an order, provide a mobile phone number, and verbally agree to receive an order confirmation text.

Verbal opt-in workflow

  1. The customer calls the restaurant to place a pickup order.
  2. The ordering assistant requests the customer's name, order items, pickup time, and mobile phone number.
  3. The assistant asks: “Would you like me to send a confirmation text message with your order number, pickup time, and total amount due to this phone number?”
  4. Only if the customer answers yes is an SMS sent.
  5. If the customer declines, no SMS is sent.

Message types

  • Order confirmation
  • Order reference number
  • Pickup time
  • Total amount due
  • Order-ready notice
  • Cancellation or service notice

Sample message

Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen

Order: LT-20260712-001
Pickup: 6:30 PM
Total due: $31.37

Please pay at the cashier when you pick up your order.

Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

Program disclosures

  • Message frequency varies.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • No marketing or promotional messages are sent.
Mobile information and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
