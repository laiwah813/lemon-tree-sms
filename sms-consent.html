SMS Program

Customers may choose to receive text updates about a pickup order they initiated by phone.

Program purpose

Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen, operated by J&A CENTURY LLC, sends transactional SMS messages only to customers who place a pickup order and verbally agree to receive text updates.

Who receives messages?

Only customers who call the restaurant, place an order, provide a mobile number, and affirmatively say yes when asked whether they would like a confirmation text.

Complete verbal opt-in workflow

  1. The customer calls Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen to place a pickup order.
  2. The ordering assistant collects the customer's name, order items, requested pickup time, and mobile phone number.
  3. The assistant asks: “Would you like me to send a confirmation text message with your order number, pickup time, and total amount due to this phone number?”
  4. Only after the customer answers yes is a text message sent.
  5. If the customer declines, no SMS is sent.

Message types

  • Order confirmation and reference number
  • Pickup time
  • Total amount due
  • Order-ready notification
  • Cancellation or service-related order update

Sample message

Lemon Tree Sushi & Ramen

Order: LT-20260712-001
Pickup: 6:30 PM
Total due: $31.37, including applicable sales tax.

Please pay at the cashier when you pick up your order.

Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

Required disclosures

  • Message frequency varies based on orders placed and order status.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • No promotional or marketing messages are sent through this program.
Mobile information, including phone numbers, text-messaging opt-in data, and consent, is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
