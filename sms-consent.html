SMS Consent

Home › SMS Consent

Customers may receive transactional texts only after verbally agreeing during a customer-initiated phone order.

How consent works

  1. The customer calls to place a pickup order.
  2. The assistant collects the customer's name, order, pickup time, and mobile number.
  3. The assistant asks whether the customer wants a confirmation text with the order number, pickup time, and total due.
  4. A text is sent only after a clear yes.
  5. If the customer declines or does not clearly agree, no customer SMS is sent.

Message types

  • Order confirmation
  • Order reference number
  • Pickup time
  • Total due
  • Order-ready or cancellation notice

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

No marketing messages are sent. Mobile information and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
